
# Dock: Features & Overview

## What is Dock?

Dock is an all-in-one personal dashboard designed for ambitious individuals who want to systematically manage their growth, productivity, and personal projects. It integrates health and fitness tracking with deep work, skill acquisition, and a strategic framework for turning ideas into monetizable products or services. It's your personal operating system for a structured and goal-oriented life.

---

## Core Pillars & Features

### 1. Dashboard: Your Daily Command Center
The main dashboard provides a high-level overview of your day and key life metrics.

- **Time-Slotted Agenda:** Schedule your day across six distinct time slots (e.g., Morning, Afternoon, Night) for structured planning.
- **Dynamic Agenda Widget:** A floating or docked list of today's scheduled tasks, complete with estimated durations.
- **Productivity Snapshot:** At-a-glance cards showing your progress in four key areas: Health, Wealth, Growth, and Direction.
- **Activity Heatmap:** A year-long visualization of your consistency, showing all completed activities.
- **Flexible Scheduling:** A calendar picker allows you to view, log, or schedule tasks for any day—past, present, or future.
- **In-Context Task Logging:** Log progress for workouts, learning, or deep work sessions directly from the dashboard agenda.

### 2. Workout Tracker: Health & Fitness Management
A complete suite for managing your physical fitness and nutrition.

- **Customizable Workout Plans:** Switch between different training splits (e.g., two-muscles-per-day or one-muscle-per-day).
- **Editable Weekly Schedules:** Fully customize the auto-populated weekly workout plans to fit your routine.
- **Comprehensive Exercise Library:** Build workouts from a default library, or add, edit, and delete your own exercises.
- **Detailed Session Logging:** Track sets, reps, and weight for every exercise.
- **Progress Visualization:** View charts and historical data for individual exercises to track your strength gains.
- **Diet Planning:** Plan meals for every day of the week, input your macros, and see your daily totals.
- **Weight & Goal Tracking:** Log weekly weigh-ins, set a goal weight, and view charts that project your path to success.

### 3. Upskill: Structured Learning & Goal Tracking
A dedicated module for acquiring and tracking new skills.

- **Skill & Topic Management:** Organize your learning into broad topics (e.g., "GPU Programming") and specific subtopics (e.g., "CUDA Fundamentals Course").
- **Goal Setting:** Define concrete goals for each topic, such as "read 500 pages" or "study for 40 hours."
- **Session Logging:** Log both the duration (minutes) and progress (pages, hours, etc.) of your study sessions.
-   **Progress Visualization:** View charts showing your cumulative progress, projecting completion dates and key milestones.
- **Learning Consistency Heatmap:** A dedicated heatmap to track your learning habits over the past year.

### 4. Deep Work: Value Creation & Project Management
The hub for applying your skills and creating value.

- **Focus Area Management:** Define your core projects or areas of deep work.
- **Topic Classification:** Classify topics as "Products" or "Services" to integrate with the monetization framework.
- **Time Tracking:** Log the exact time spent on each focus area.
- **Skill Integration:** Link your "Upskill" tasks to "Deep Work" projects, creating a clear map from learning to application.
- **Branding Pipeline:** Mark completed focus areas as "Ready for Branding" to queue them up for content creation in the Personal Branding module.

### 5. Monetization Engine: Turning Skills into Income
A framework to systematically convert your work into tangible offers.

-   **Personal Branding:**
    -   **Content Bundling:** Group related focus areas into content bundles for articles, videos, or social media posts.
    -   **Creation Workflow:** Track content creation through a 4-stage process: Create, Optimize, Review, and Final Review.
    -   **Publishing Checklist:** Track where each bundle has been shared (X/Twitter, LinkedIn, DEV.to).
-   **Lead Generation & Offer System:**
    -   Define and track daily outreach activities (e.g., "Cold DM 2 people").
    -   Define and track the development of your core service offerings (e.g., "Define freelance services").

### 6. Strategic Planning: From Idea to Launch
High-level tools to visualize your entire strategy and roadmap.

-   **Productization & Offerization:**
    -   **Strategic Definition:** Turn Deep Work topics into well-defined product or service plans.
    -   **Guided Gap Analysis:** Use a structured form to identify market needs (e.g., Skill Gap, Execution Gap) and define your unique value proposition.
    -   **Release Planning:** Create detailed, versioned roadmaps for your products/services with launch dates, features, and descriptions.
-   **Strategic Matrix:** A consolidated dashboard showing all your defined products and services, summarizing their format, status, and strategic goals.
-   **Interactive Mind Map:**
    -   A hierarchical visualization of your entire Dock, from high-level strategies down to individual learning tasks.
    -   Shows real-time status of every task: Scheduled for Today, Logged Today, Pending from Past, and Completed in Past.

## System-Wide Features

- **Authentication:** Secure local user registration and login, plus a pre-populated `demo/demo` account for exploration.
- **Data Persistence:** All user data is automatically saved to the browser's local storage.
- **Cloud Sync:** Securely push your local data to the cloud (Vercel Blob) and pull it down on any other device.
- **Data Portability:** Export your entire Dock data to a single JSON file for backup, or import a previous backup to restore your state.
- **Theming:** Choose from multiple visual themes, including Default, Matrix, and Ad Dark.
- **Focus Aids:** An optional, ambient background audio player to help with focus and concentration.
-   **Weekly Backup Reminders:** A friendly prompt on Mondays to encourage you to back up your data.
